Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2025

1. Introduction

Flow Timer is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

2. Information We Don't Collect

Flow Timer is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect usage statistics
  • We do not store any user data on external servers

3. Local Data Storage

All app data, including:

  • Timer settings
  • Categories
  • Theme preferences
  • App icon preferences

are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted externally.

4. Third-Party Services

Flow Timer does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms.

5. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at flowtimer@divpuri.co.uk.
